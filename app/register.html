Welcome to Risk, an ERC20 based team game where you can join the never ending battle of Red vs Blue!

To play, you must first register with a team below. In order to participate you must purchase some Risk (RSK) tokens. The RSK token is an integral part of the game - you will battle against the other team by riskign tokens each round. The teams who risked the most tokens goes home as the winner - getting their tokens back and 50% of the other teams tokens (The other 50% are burned).
